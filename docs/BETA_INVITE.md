# Private Beta Invite Copy

Subject: You are invited to test Breakup OS Dating

Hi,

I am opening a small private beta for Breakup OS Dating, and I would love your help testing it.

Breakup OS started as a private relationship intelligence tool: a place to track situations, patterns, mixed signals, no-contact recovery, emotional investment, and AI-assisted next steps. The dating beta adds a Bumble-style layer on top: dating profiles, discovery, likes, matches, chat, safety controls, and the ability to convert a match into a Breakup OS situation for deeper tracking.

This is early-stage software. Some parts are polished, some parts are still intentionally simple, and your feedback will directly shape what gets improved before a wider launch.

## What To Try

- Create a dating profile with a nickname.
- Upload a safe profile photo.
- Browse Discovery.
- Like or pass on profiles.
- Match with another test user.
- Send chat messages.
- Try reporting and blocking.
- Try the Safety Center.
- If you have Pro access, try AI reply help, icebreakers, reports, weekly summary, and message analysis.
- Convert a match into a Breakup OS situation.
- Export your data.
- Delete your test data.

## Safety And Privacy

Please treat this as a beta, not a vault for highly sensitive real-life content.

Do not enter:

- Full legal names
- Addresses
- Phone numbers
- Financial details
- Explicit images
- Details involving minors
- Crisis, self-harm, legal, medical, or emergency situations
- Anything you would not want a small beta product team to help debug responsibly

Breakup OS Dating is not therapy, legal advice, crisis support, or a replacement for trusted professional help. AI outputs can be wrong, incomplete, or too confident.

## How To Report Bugs

Send:

- What you were trying to do
- What happened
- What you expected to happen
- Browser and device
- Screenshot or short screen recording if possible
- The account email you used for testing

Please avoid sending screenshots that include sensitive private messages.

## Feedback Questions

1. Did onboarding make sense?
2. Did the dating profile feel easy to complete?
3. Did Discovery feel clear and safe?
4. Did matching and chat behave the way you expected?
5. Did the safety/report/block flows feel visible enough?
6. Were any AI features useful, confusing, or uncomfortable?
7. Did anything feel too intense, too clinical, or too playful?
8. What would make you trust the product more?
9. What would make you pay for Pro?
10. What should be fixed before inviting more people?

Thank you for helping test this early version. Honest feedback is more useful than polite feedback.
